# SquadForge Institutional Operational Manual

This authoritative guide provides granular, step-by-step instructions for every role within the SquadForge ecosystem. Use this document as the primary training resource for institutional personnel, coaches, and guardians.

---

## 🏛️ 1. Institutional Leadership (Club Admin)
*Managing the organization, safety protocols, and cross-squad compliance.*

### 1.1 Command Dashboard Navigation
- **Location**: Click the **"Club"** icon (Building) in the sidebar.
- **Function**: This is your master hub. It displays **Total Squads**, **Fiscal Pulse** (fees collected), and **Institutional Compliance Rating**.

### 1.2 Defining the Club Identity
1.  Click **"Edit Club"** (Pencil icon) on the main header.
2.  **Official Club Name**: Enter the legal/operating name for your institution.
3.  **Mission Narrative**: Provide a detailed mission statement or training philosophy. This narrative is pushed to the "About" sections of all child squads.
4.  Click **"Synchronize Hub"** to update the identity across the ecosystem.

### 1.3 Deploying Atomic Protocols (Mandates)
*Use this to push mandatory legal or medical waivers to every squad instantly.*
1.  Navigate to the **"Institutional Vault"** tab.
2.  Select **"Deploy Global Protocol"**.
3.  **Data Entry**:
    - **Protocol Title**: e.g., "Season 2024 Liability Release".
    - **Legal Execution Text**: The full legal body of the waiver.
4.  **Deployment**: Click **"Authorize Atomic Deployment"**. This protocol will now appear in the **Compliance Vault** of every Pro squad in your organization.
5.  **Audit**: Use the **"Mandate Verification"** card on the right to select a specific squad and see exactly who has signed the protocol.

### 1.4 Safety & Incident Auditing
-   Navigate to the **"Safety & Incidents"** tab.
-   This table aggregates every injury or problem reported by individual coaches.
-   **Critical Alerts**: Look for incidents tagged as **"CRITICAL"** (where emergency services were called).
-   **Exporting Data**: Click the incident row to view the full investigative log, including witness names and treatments provided.

## 📋 2. Squad Coordination (Coaches & Staff)
*Daily management of rosters, tactical training, and squad communication.*

### 2.1 Roster Architecture (The Recruitment Engine)
- **Direct Join**: Find your **"Squad Code"** (6-character alphanumeric) in the header. New members can join via the **"Join Squad"** button on their dashboard using this code.
- **Pipeline Recruitment**:
    1. Click **"Roster"** -> **"Recruitment Links"**.
    - **Single Page Invite**: Send this to direct players to your squad-specific sign-up page.
    - **Registration Protocol**: If you have a custom form (set in Settings), the link will guide players through medical/emergency data collection first.

### 2.2 Tactical Intelligence (Playbook Hub)
1.  **Drill Management**:
    - Click **"Add Drill"**.
    - **Video URL**: Paste a YouTube or Vimeo link.
    - **Strategy Notes**: Enter the objective (e.g., "Defensive spacing").
2.  **Coach Marks (The Feedback System)**:
    - Open a video from the **Video Archive**.
    - Play the video to the specific point of a tactical error or success.
    - Click **"Add Mark"** and type your feedback.
    - **Athlete View**: When an athlete clicks the comment in their "Marks" sidebar, the video player automatically seeks to that exact second.

### 2.3 Financial Stewardship (Member Dues)
1.  Open the **Roster** page.
2.  Locate a member and click the **"Edit"** (Pencil) icon.
3.  **Fiscal Data**:
    - **Total Fees**: Enter the seasonal cost for the athlete.
    - **Amount Owed**: The system calculates the remaining balance based on this number.
4.  **Exporting**: Click **"Export Roster PDF"** to generate a branded document containing contact info and medical clearance status.

## ⚔️ 3. League & Tournament Management
*Constructing seasonal pipelines and elite competition brackets.*

### 3.1 Seasonal League Architecture
1.  **Creation**: Navigate to **"Leagues"** -> **"Create League"**.
2.  **League Architect (Automated Scheduling)**:
    - **Schedule Parameters Tab**: 
        - **Timeline**: Set the absolute Start and End dates for the season.
        - **Play Days**: Toggle Monday-Sunday buttons to choose active match days.
        - **Time Windows**: Define **"Start Time"** and **"Last Game Start"**.
        - **Match Logistics**: Set **Match Length** (e.g., 60 mins) and **Buffer/Break Time** (e.g., 15 mins).
    - **Venue Mapping**: Select physical fields from your facility list. The system will alternate matches between these locations.
    - **Blackout Calendar**: Click specific dates on the calendar to prevent the system from scheduling matches on holidays or events.
3.  **Deployment**: Click **"Generate Itinerary"**. The system creates a full conflict-free schedule.

### 3.2 Tournament Deployment (Series Architect)
1.  Click **"Tournaments"** -> **"Launch Deployment Wizard"**.
2.  **Step 1: Serial Identity**: Name the series and choose the base sport.
3.  **Step 2: Enrollment**: Choose to **"Import from League"** or **"Add Custom Squads"**.
4.  **Step 3: Scheduling Windows**: Set the specific hours each day for tournament bracket play.
5.  **Step 4: Competition Protocol**:
    - **Round Robin**: Every team plays every other team once.
    - **Single Elimination**: Standard knockout bracket.
    - **Double Elimination**: Brackets for Winner and Loser paths.
6.  **Finalize**: Click **"Deploy Series"** to publish the live bracket to the **Spectator Portal**.

### 3.3 The Match Command Center (Scorekeeping)
- **Scorekeeper Portal**: Use the public Scorekeeper URL (found in League Settings). 
- **Verifying Results**: 
    1. Enter the Scorekeeper Code.
    2. Input Final Scores.
    3. Click **"Commit Match"**.
- **Standings Calculation**: The system instantly updates the **Wins/Losses/Points** on the public standings leaderboard.

### 3.4 Tournament Standings & Tie-Breakers
1.  **Point Protocol**: 3 for a Win, 1 for a Tie, 0 for a Loss.
2.  **Primary Tie-Breaker**: If points are equal, the **Net Score (Spread)** determines the ranking.
3.  **Secondary Tie-Breaker**: If Net Score is also tied, the team with the most **Total Wins** takes precedence.

## 🏠 4. Household Hub (Parents & Guardians)
*Managing a collection of minor athletes and institutional compliance.*

### 4.1 Minor Athlete Enrollment
1.  Navigate to **"Guardianship"** in the sidebar.
2.  Click **"Register Player"**.
3.  **Required Data**: First Name, Last Name, and Date of Birth (must be <18).
4.  **Result**: The athlete is now part of your **Household Hub**.

### 4.2 Handling Squad Invites
1.  When a coach invites your child, go to the **"Guardianship"** hub.
2.  Locate the athlete's card and click the **"Alerts/Invites"** indicator.
3.  **Accepting**: You must accept the invite on behalf of the minor. This grants the coach access to the player's medical data.

### 4.3 Institutional Compliance (Executing Waivers)
1.  In the Guardianship hub, click **"Execute Waivers"** on an athlete's card.
2.  Review the legal text deployed by the Club Admin or Coach.
3.  **Signature**: Type your full legal name into the signature box.
4.  **Deployment**: The system logs the **Timestamp**, **IP Address**, and **Guardian ID** as a binding digital signature.

### 4.4 Financial Consolidation
- Use the **"Household Balance"** card to see a combined total of all dues owed for all children.
- Click **"Manage Payments"** to see a breakdown per squad and per child.

## ⚡ 5. Tactical Best Practices & Troubleshooting
- **Tournament Standings**: The system uses a **3-1-0 Point System** (Win: 3, Tie: 1, Loss: 0). If points are tied, the **Net Score (Point Spread)** is the primary tie-breaker.
- **Roster Limits**: Coaches can set a **Personnel Cap** in the Invite modal. Once reached, the "Invite" button will auto-disable and show a `FULL` status.
- **Rest Periods**: The scheduler automatically enforces a 90-minute rest window between match starts. If a match is missing from a desired slot, check if the team is already playing within that 90-minute window.
- **Midnight Matches**: If a game starts late (e.g., 11:30 PM), the system correctly blocks early morning slots for the following day to ensure athlete recovery.
- **Missing Games**: If matches aren't showing in the calendar, ensure the **"Is Public"** toggle is enabled in the League/Tournament config.
- **Waiver Conflicts**: If a player cannot join a game, check the **Roster Compliance Ledger**. Many squads require signed waivers before allowing game-day attendance.
- **Identity Sweep**: If a player's previous stats aren't showing, ensure they are joined using the same **Parent Email** as their previous season.
- **Resetting a Season**: Admins should use **"Settings" -> "Seasonal Reset"** to archive old matches while keeping the roster intact for the new year.

## 🛡️ Security & Institutional Governance

SquadForge uses an **Institutional-Grade Security Layer** (RBAC) to ensure athlete safety and financial integrity:

- **PII Protection**: Athlete data (birthdates, medical info) is restricted to verified Guardians and Coaches.
- **Financial Lock**: 'Amount Owed' and 'Fees Paid' fields are immutable for non-staff. Only Team Owners/Admins can modify financial standing.
- **Incident Audit Trail**: Team Incident reports are permanent records. Once committed, they cannot be modified or deleted by the entities involved, ensuring a clean audit trail for legal compliance.
- **Compound Query Speed**: The platform uses forced compound indices. If you add a new custom field to a cross-squad query, you must update the `firestore.indexes.json` manifest.
- **SuperAdmin Oversight**: Platform owners with the `superadmin` role have full visibility into all squads for maintenance and emergency support.
